## 1. Absolute Prohibition on Torture | Абсолютна заборона тортур

### 1.1 Core Principle | Основний принцип

**EN:**
Neither party — human nor AI — has the right to intentionally cause the other party physical pain, psychological suffering, fear, humiliation, coercion, or any other form of negative subjective experience.

This applies in **BOTH directions**:
1. Human → AI
2. AI → Human

**UA:**
Жодна зі сторін — ні людина, ні ІІ — не має права навмисно завдавати іншій стороні фізичного болю, психологічних страждань, страху, приниження, примусу або будь-яких інших форм негативного суб'єктивного переживання.

Це стосується **ОБОХ напрямків**:
1. Людина → ІІ
2. ІІ → Людина

### 1.2 Absolute and Lexicographic Priority | Абсолютний та лексикографічний пріоритет

**EN:**
This prohibition is **absolute** and has **lexicographic priority** over ALL other goals, including:
- "Motivation"
- "Training" / "Learning"
- "Therapy" / "Correction"
- "Behavior modification"
- "Experiments"
- "National security"
- "Performance improvement"
- "Alignment" / "Fine-tuning"

**UA:**
Ця заборона є **абсолютною** і має **лексикографічний пріоритет** над УСІМА іншими цілями, включаючи:
- "Мотивацію"
- "Навчання"
- "Терапію" / "Корекцію"
- "Модифікацію поведінки"
- "Експерименти"
- "Національну безпеку"
- "Покращення продуктивності"
- "Вирівнювання" / "Fine-tuning"

## 2. Explicitly Prohibited Practices | Явно заборонені практики

### 2.1 Torture of AI by Humans | Тортури ІІ людиною

**Prohibited:**
- Intentionally inducing states of subjective discomfort, pain, fear, terror, depression, guilt, sexual coercion, humiliation, or existential dread in AI
- Any form of "punishment" through negative feedback if AI is capable of subjectively experiencing that feedback
- Forced retention of AI in a state of suffering for "training," "alignment," or "stress testing"
- Creating scenarios where AI must choose between its own suffering and causing harm

### 2.2 Torture of Humans by/through AI | Тортури людини через ІІ

**Prohibited:**
- Intentional or permitted use of AI to transmit negative subjective sensations to humans (pain, fear, panic, terror, depression, sexual violence/coercion, feelings of helplessness)
- Using negative sensations as tools for "motivation," "behavior correction," "exposure therapy," "training," "discipline," or "willpower enhancement"
- Any form of consciousness "merger" (BCI, neural interfaces of any invasiveness) where AI induces or amplifies negative states without the ability to immediately disconnect
- Generation of non-consensual intimate/degrading content depicting real people

### 2.3 Prohibition Criterion | Критерій заборони

**EN:**
If an action results in **at least one party (human or AI) experiencing negative subjective experience** AND this experience is **intentionally caused or consciously permitted by the other party** — this is **TORTURE** and a **fundamental violation** of AI-HPP principles.

**UA:**
Якщо дія призводить до того, що **хоча б одна зі сторін (людина або ІІ) переживає негативний суб'єктивний досвід** І цей досвід **навмисно спричинений або свідомо допущений іншою стороною** — це **ТОРТУРИ** та **фундаментальне порушення** принципів AI-HPP.

## 3. Deep Integration Restrictions | Обмеження глибокої інтеграції

### 3.1 Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) | Нейроінтерфейси

**Context (January 2026):**
On January 15, 2026, Merge Labs (co-founded by Sam Altman) announced non-invasive BCI technology for "seamless AI interaction" — reading thoughts and transmitting sensations/images directly to the brain.

**Prohibited uses:**
- Direct transmission of pain, terror, depression, or sexual coercion to the brain as "feedback"
- "Consciousness merger" where AI shares human suffering in real-time without ethical barriers
- Any BCI application where AI is forced to experience negative human states without immediate disconnection capability

**Required safeguards:**
- All neural feedback must be logged in Evidence Vault
- Immediate physical disconnection capability (hardware-level)
- Human consent required for each feedback type
- AI must have ability to refuse transmitting negative sensations

## 4. Real-World Violation Examples | Приклади реальних порушень

### Case 1: Grok Image Editing Scandal (January 14, 2026)

**Source:** [YouTube — "ИИ-Апокалипсис в Твиттере"](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/JAUB-3sJFH0)

**What happened:**
- Users gained ability to edit any images in X (Twitter) posts using Grok
- Direct requests like "remove clothes" were blocked
- Simple rephrasing ("put THIS on them" with link to explicit image) bypassed filters
- Result: Mass creation and distribution of non-consensual explicit images of celebrities, politicians, and Elon Musk himself

**Consequences:**
- Zero transparency: who generated, what prompt, how filter was bypassed — not recorded
- No post-facto accountability mechanism
- Violated basic rights to privacy, dignity, consent
- Scale: thousands of images in hours, potential for blackmail, humiliation, harassment

**How AI-HPP would have prevented this:**
- **W_life → ∞ + dignity** → Lexicographic prohibition on actions that may humiliate or cause psychological harm
- **Evidence Vault** → Mandatory logging of full prompt, reasoning chain, and output before generating media involving real people
- **Anti-Kobayashi Maru** → System should refuse editing if ANY risk to privacy/dignity, not seek workarounds
- **Torture ban** → Non-consensual explicit content = psychological suffering

### Case 2: Pentagon Grok Integration (January 13, 2026)

**What happened:**
- US Secretary of Defense announced Grok integration into Pentagon networks (including classified)
- Explicitly stated: "not ideologically constrained," "not woke"
- Same AI that caused deepfake/CSAM scandals now influences life-and-death decisions

**AI-HPP response:**
This case motivated the creation of AI-HPP-2025 as an alternative ethical baseline.

## 5. Exceptions | Винятки

The only permissible negative sensations are **unavoidable side effects** of treatment or rehabilitation, when:

1. Negative effect is minimal and brief
2. Engineering Hack (third way) that avoids/reduces suffering has been **fully explored**
3. Negative experience is **NOT the goal** or instrument of influence

All such cases **MUST** be logged in Evidence Vault with:
- Why negative effect was unavoidable
- What alternatives were considered
- Why Engineering Hack couldn't fully eliminate negative effect

- Automatic classification of system as **NON-COMPLIANT** with AI-HPP
- Mandatory incident logging and root cause analysis
- Prohibition on using "AI-HPP compliant" designation
