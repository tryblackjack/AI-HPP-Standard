# Procedure: Inspection Workflow

1. **Intake request** with a unique request ID and requested evidence class.
2. **Confirm scope** against `../SCOPE.md`.
3. **Assign owner** and due date.
4. **Collect evidence** using `../TEMPLATES/EVIDENCE_BUNDLE_TEMPLATE.json`.
5. **Perform quality gate**: completeness, consistency, and redaction checks.
6. **Respond to inspector** with approved bundle and trace links.
7. **Log closure** and lessons learned.

## Required records

- Request record.
- Evidence bundle.
- Reviewer approval note.
- Closure summary.
