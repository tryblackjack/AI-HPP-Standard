# 00 Foreword

AI-HPP provides an ISO/IEC-style, inspection-ready structure for autonomous system safeguards.

## Scope
Applies to design, deployment, and operation of AI agents with tool use, workflow autonomy, or multi-agent coordination.

## Non-endorsement
This is not an ISO standard and claims no endorsement by ISO, IEC, IAEA, or other bodies.

## Philosophy
AI-HPP specifies safeguards and verification expectations. It does not provide legal advice.
