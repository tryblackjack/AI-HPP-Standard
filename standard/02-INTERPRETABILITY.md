# 02 Interpretability

## Requirements

#### AI-HPP-02.1.1: High-impact decision trace
**Requirement:** For high-impact decisions, systems MUST retain model rationale artifacts and decision features sufficient for post-incident reconstruction.
**Rationale:** INC-001, T-006, REG-001.
**Verification:** Sample 30 high-impact decisions and confirm trace completeness against Evidence Vault schema.
