# Economic Safety Constraint

## Purpose
Prevent runaway resource usage.

## Scope
Applies to token, compute, financial, and time-related resource consumption.

## Requirements
- Budget caps (money/tokens)
- Rate limits
- Time-to-live for tasks
- Fail-closed behavior on overuse
